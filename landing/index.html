How it works

1
Play a film. Nyx arms automatically when video starts.
2
Its camera model watches your eyes — on your Mac, never uploaded.
3
Eyes closed too long → a quiet nudge, then playback pauses.
4
Still asleep → Nyx sleeps your Mac. Move or open your eyes to cancel.
5
In the morning, see exactly where you dozed off.

Your face never leaves your Mac

Nyx analyzes the webcam in memory and discards every frame. It stores only your calibration threshold, settings, and a short text recap — all local, all offline.
